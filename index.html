About SoulTide

SoulTide was founded during Charleston Start up Weekend 2017. Our goal is to take recycled ocean plastic and turn it into a universal flip flop that is both comfortable and sustainable. SoulTide's founders are: Matthieu Leo, Hannah Sofer, Elmano Hamilton, Samantha Campedelli and Andre Wilson.

The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is now almost the size of mainland United States and it continually growing. If the millennial generation does not act the worlds oceans will die and we will die with it.
